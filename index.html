Our Story

Founded in 2005, Heebaan Garden has been serving authentic dishes made with love and the freshest ingredients. Our chefs bring decades of experience to create memorable dining experiences for our guests.

We pride ourselves on preserving traditional cooking methods while incorporating modern techniques to deliver exceptional flavors that keep our customers coming back.

Our restaurant features a beautiful garden setting where you can enjoy your meals surrounded by nature, creating a peaceful dining atmosphere unlike any other.
